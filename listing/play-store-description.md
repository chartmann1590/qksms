# TextPilot - Play Store Listing

## App Description

TextPilot is a next-generation SMS & MMS messaging app for Android that blends powerful messaging essentials with AI-assisted replies, web browser access, and advanced customization. Whether you're sending texts, photos, or group messages, TextPilot brings speed, intelligence, and flexibility to your everyday conversations.

TextPilot is based on the open-source QKSMS project, enhanced with optional AI Smart Reply features and Web Sync capabilities that let you leverage your own self-hosted infrastructure for intelligent replies and web-based messaging.
